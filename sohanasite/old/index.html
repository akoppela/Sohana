About me

Anna Sohana Russia

Skills: Yoga Massage Meditation Dance

In 2000 I graduated with two bachelor’s degrees (in Finance and Credit) from the Moscow School of Economics (HSE) and the Moscow State University of Economics, Statistics and Informatics (MESI). I had worked for companies such as OJSC Capital Insurance and the Hephaestus insurance company.

My work did not bring me much joy, and I began a search of myself in other areas. Yoga became the gateway to a new realm, as a good friend and a practice which changed my life.

My journey in yoga began in 2007. I started practicing Ashtanga yoga with a certified teacher, Dmitri Baryshnikov (Moscow). Practice brought calm, discipline and self-confidence into my life. Then, I decided to immerse myself further in my practice and went to India. There I was trained by a certified teacher of Sri Patabhi Joyce - Lino Miele (Italy), and later discovered the Iyengar style. I was trained by certified BKS Iyengar teachers: Sharat Arora (Himalayas), Gabriella Jubilyaro (Italy), and Tatiana Tolochkova (Auroville, India). In 2014 I started my practice in Universal Yoga and Dance of Shiva. I completed the Universal Yoga teacher’s course in 2014 (300 hours) and 2 levels of Dance of Shiva. I have been teaching yoga since 2011.

All the years of my practice I was in search of a teacher to organize and complement my knowledge, making it orderly and efficient, and developing my perspective. My search ended with a meeting with Andrey Lappa (Universal Yoga). So much thanks to him, for changing not only my practice, but my attitude to life.

In 2009, I first became acquainted with massage, which I studied in India, unaware of my growing ability to work with the body and energy. Learning one style of massage created further curiosity and a desire to learn others, and my hobby grew into a serious job. Currently I`m a certified professional masseuse. I have studied with masters in Thailand (ITM - International Training Massage school), Tibet (Swami Darshi), China (Universal Tao Center), USA (Satyarthi Deva) and Russia (Institute of Professional massage).

Since 2005, I have practiced Osho meditation. My teacher, Swami Dashi, who was himself, a student of the Indian sannyasin Bhagwan Shree Rajneesh Osho, opened a vast world of energy practices for me that have led to a deep understanding of unconditional love and the concept of totality. Later I took sannyasa (Osho’s teachings on fidelity) and received the sannyasa name Deva Sohana, meaning "divine beauty". Since 2010, I have been conducting meditation sessions, rejoicing for those with the desire to change their lives and know true feelings of love and compassion. In 2011, I was trained in Stanislav Grof’s Holotropic Breathwork. This method of transpersonal therapy is one of the most efficient and fastest psychotherapies around today.

My wish is that you love yourself, live your life with ease and serve the world for the benefit of all sentient beings - "Sarva Mangalam."
